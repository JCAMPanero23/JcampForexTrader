## üîç How to Verify Broker Quality

### Before Depositing Real Money

**1. Check Regulation**
- Visit broker website ‚Üí "About Us" ‚Üí "Regulation"
- Verify license numbers on regulator websites:
  - ASIC (Australia): https://asic.gov.au
  - FCA (UK): https://register.fca.org.uk
  - CySEC (Cyprus): https://www.cysec.gov.cy

**2. Test Demo Spreads**
- Compare demo spreads to advertised rates
- Monitor during London/NY hours (09:00-22:00 UTC+2)
- Check during news events (do spreads spike to 500+ pips?)

**3. Read Reviews**
- Trustpilot: Search "[Broker Name] review"
- Forex Peace Army: Check complaints
- Reddit: Search "r/forex [Broker Name]"

**4. Test Withdrawal (Small Deposit)**
- Deposit $100-200 initially
- Make a few trades
- Withdraw $50 after 1 week
- Verify withdrawal is fast and free

**Red Flags:**
- ‚ùå Delayed withdrawals (> 5 business days)
- ‚ùå Withdrawal fees (should be free)
- ‚ùå "Trading volume requirements" before withdrawal
- ‚ùå Account suddenly closed/restricted
